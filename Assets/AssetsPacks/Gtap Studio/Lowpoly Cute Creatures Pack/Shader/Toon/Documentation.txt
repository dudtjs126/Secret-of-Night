1. Property "MainTex" - basic material texture
2. Property "ColorOffset" - offset of additional shades obtained using the "ColorSteps" property
3. Property "HighlightIntesity" - allows you to highlight the intensity of light areas by increasing the size of the area
4. Property "ColorSteps" - affects the number of additional shades of the primary color
5. Property "ShadedColor" - color  of shadow areas
6. Property "LitColor" - color of illuminated places
7. Property "HighlightColor" - color of highlight areas
